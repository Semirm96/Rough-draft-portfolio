About Me

Hi, My name is Semir Mujkic and I have been an electrician for 5 years. Throughout my time as an electrician I have decided that while I am good at what I do I've decided I want to try my hand at making a career change to something I'd be able to enjoy much more than what I have now. I've grown to be a worker that constantly works on perfecting every task I receive to the best of my ability and to never stop learning new and faster ways to achieve the projected end result within a timely manner.

In my time I've also grown to enjoy alot of things related to computers, like coding, playing games, and overall just enjoying all there is to offer on the internet and I figured I could also enjoy a career in being a fullstack web developer. My time off the computer and work basically revolves spending time with my wife and son who is 2 years old at this point so my life is pretty much always occupied and making this career change would give me a valid excuse to be on computers more, which makes it an even better idea for me in the long run.
